Pillars of Islam: Understand and Learn Them Through Online Quran Lessons

All praise is due to Allah, who has guided us to Islam, the complete way of life. We praise Him, seek His help, and ask for His forgiveness. We seek refuge in Allah from the evil of our own selves and from our wrong actions.

Dear brothers and sisters, the foundation of Islam rests on five essential pillars that every Muslim must uphold. These pillars are the declaration of faith (Shahada), prayer (Salah), fasting (Sawm), charity (Zakat), and pilgrimage (Hajj).

Each of these pillars represents a vital aspect of our relationship with Allah and with one another. They are acts of worship, submission, and devotion that shape our faith and character.

With the advancements of modern technology, online Quran lessons have become an excellent way to deepen our understanding of these pillars. By studying the Quran and Hadith, we can learn the profound wisdom behind each of these obligations and how to perform them correctly.

The Quran repeatedly emphasizes the importance of these pillars. For example, regarding prayer, Allah says: “Indeed, prayer has been decreed upon the believers a decree of specified times.” [Quran 4:103]. Similarly, fasting is described as a means of attaining Taqwa: “O you who have believed, decreed upon you is fasting as it was decreed upon those before you that you may become righteous.” [Quran 2:183].

Dear believers, make it a priority to learn and practice these pillars consistently. Use online resources and platforms to enhance your understanding and ensure that your worship is in accordance with the teachings of Islam.

We ask Allah to grant us the ability to fulfill these pillars sincerely and consistently, and to make us among those who uphold Islam in its true spirit.

And our final prayer is: All praise is due to Allah, Lord of the worlds.
