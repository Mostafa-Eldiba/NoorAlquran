Foundations of Islam: Essential Beliefs & Practices

Introduction:

A comprehensive journey through Islam's core tenets, designed to strengthen faith and clarify religious obligations through authentic Quranic and Hadith-based teachings.

Key Content:
Pillars of Faith:

In-depth study of Tawhid, Angels, Divine Books, and Qadar

Comparative analysis of Islamic vs. modern ideologies

Practical Worship:

Step-by-step guides to Salah, Sawm, Zakat, and Hajj

Fiqh of daily life: Halal/Haram, Islamic finance basics

Prophetic Legacy:

Seerah timeline from pre-Islam to Khulafa Rashideen

Analysis of 40 Nawawi Hadiths

Target Audience:

New Muslims, reverts, and anyone seeking structured Islamic knowledge

Conclusion:

Students will gain confidence in practicing Islam authentically while understanding its philosophical depth.

Advanced Islamic Theology: Exploring Depth

Introduction:

For deep thinkers and knowledge seekers, this course delves into complex theological concepts while maintaining alignment with Ahl al-Sunnah principles.

Key Content:
Historical Debates:

Analysis of classical schools: Ash'ari vs. Maturidi theology

Critical study of sectarian deviations

Quranic Sciences:

Ulum al-Quran: Asbab al-Nuzul, Nasikh wa Mansukh

Modern scientific miracles in Quran analysis

Philosophical Inquiry:

Islam's response to existential questions

Comparative religion: Abrahamic faiths analysis

Target Audience:

Islamic students, university researchers, critical thinkers

Conclusion:

Participants will develop scholarly approaches to contemporary challenges while preserving traditional wisdom.

Islamic Adventures: Learning Through Play (Kids)

Introduction:

An interactive Islamic education program where children explore faith through games, animated stories, and creative projects.

Key Content:
Quranic Heroes:

Animated series on Prophet stories with moral dilemmas

Role-playing Islamic history events

Faith Crafts:

Build your own Kaaba model workshops

Arabic calligraphy art sessions

Ethics in Action:

"Good Deeds Tracker" mobile app with rewards

Community service projects for kids

Target Audience:

Children 5-15 years, Islamic weekend schools

Conclusion:

Young learners will embrace Islamic values joyfully while building strong faith foundations.
