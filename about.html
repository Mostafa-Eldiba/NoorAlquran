Noor Al Quran is a distinguished online Quran academy dedicated to providing professional and comprehensive Quranic education through virtual classes. With a team of highly qualified and experienced instructors, we offer a transformative learning experience for students of all ages. Mostafa Eldib

President

Teaching the Quran online has become a powerful tool for spreading Islamic knowledge globally, especially for non-native speakers. It offers a flexible and accessible way to learn the Quran, Tajweed, and Islamic principles, regardless of geographical boundaries. This approach not only helps non-Arabic speakers understand the Quran in their native languages but also connects them with certified teachers from Al-Azhar and other reputable institutions. By embracing online Quran education, Muslims worldwide can strengthen their faith, deepen their understanding of Islam's pillars, and foster a stronger connection to the teachings of the Quran. This modern method ensures that the message of Islam reaches every corner of the world, making Quranic education inclusive and adaptable to everyone's needs.
